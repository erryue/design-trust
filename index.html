Description  实验描述

The experiment studies the the effects of quality and diversity of AI results during creative design process. The experiment includes two design tasks, which will be completed separately with the assistance of the Carvizio and UIHue systems. Each task consists of four parts: a tutorial, a formal task, a questionnaire, and an interview.

本实验旨在研究人工智能辅助设计结果的质量和多样性对设计过程的影响。本实验包含两个设计任务，您将在Carvizio和UIHue系统的辅助下分别完成。 每个任务包含四个部分：教程，正式任务，问卷和访谈。


Time and payments  时间与报酬

Approximately 1 hour, 60 RMB allowances after study.

实验时间约为1小时, 完成实验后将获得60元报酬。


Participant's rights  参与者权利

You may ask any study-related questions before and during the study. You may take breaks as needed and stop your participation at any time. You have the right to refuse to answer questions. The results of this study may be published in academic journals/conferences/books. However, your name or other identifiers will not be used without your specific permission.

在实验过程中您有任何问题都可以直接提出。实验过程中，您可以根据需要进行休息调整，在感觉研究内容不适等情况下您也可以随时终止实验。如果问题涉及到相关隐私，您可以拒绝回答。 本研究的结果可能会在学术期刊/会议/书籍上发表，但在未征得您本人同意的情况下，您的姓名或其他可以确认身份的信息不会出现在发表物中。
