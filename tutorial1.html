Task 1 overview  任务一概述

In this task, you will engage in a creative exercise where you imagine a future concept based on a given theme. Your goal is to generate as many sketches as possible within a limited time and come up with a variety of creative ideas.

在这个任务中，您将参与一项创意练习，围绕指定的主题进行未来概念的设计构思。您的目标是在规定时间内尽可能多地生成草图，提出多样创意。

Example: "Design a personal assistant robot in the future."

示例: "设计一个未来的个人助理机器人。"

Complete your sketches on the iPad.

在iPad画板上完成您的草图。
