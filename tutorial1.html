Task 1 overview  任务一概述

In this task, you will engage in a creative exercise where you imagine a future concept based on a given theme. Your goal is to generate as many sketches as possible within a limited time and come up with a variety of creative ideas.

在这个任务中，您将参与一项创意练习，围绕指定的主题进行未来概念的设计构思。您的目标是在规定时间内尽可能多地生成草图，提出多样创意。

Example: "Design a personal assistant robot in the future."

示例: "设计一个未来的个人助理机器人。"

Carvizio introduction  系统介绍

Carvizio is an AI-powered image generation system that assists with concept design. With Carvizio, you can generate four design images each time, with a maximum of four sessions. In addition to completing the design task, you will also need to score each set of generated results.

Carvizio 是一个 AI 驱动的图像生成系统，可以帮助您进行概念设计。使用 Carvizio，您每次可以生成四张设计图像，最多生成结果四次。除完成设计任务外，您还需要对每组生成的结果打分。
